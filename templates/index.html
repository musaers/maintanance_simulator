About Maintenance Optimization

Maintenance optimization involves determining the optimal schedule and strategy for maintaining equipment to minimize costs and downtime. This system simulates a scenario where multiple components can degrade over time, and maintenance decisions need to be made based on the system's status signals.

The Signal System

Green Signal (0)

All components are in perfect condition. No maintenance is required.

Yellow Signal (1)

Some components are degraded but not failed. Preventive maintenance may be required.

Red Signal (2)

At least one component has failed. Immediate corrective maintenance is required.

Maintenance Strategies

Use this simulator to experiment with different parameters and understand the trade-offs between preventive and corrective maintenance strategies. Key factors include:

  • Cost balance between preventive and corrective maintenance
  • Optimal timing for preventive maintenance
  • Component degradation probabilities and patterns
  • System performance metrics and cost analysis
